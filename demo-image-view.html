ImageView Component

A versatile image component with support for different sizing modes, positioning, and visual effects.

Modes

Cover (Default)
Contain
Stretch

1:1 Mode with Positioning

In 1:1 mode, the image is displayed at its natural size (or scaled if needed) but not forced to fill. Positioning controls visible area.

Visual Effects (FX)

Bokeh (Blur)
Pan Left
Pan Right
Zoom In
